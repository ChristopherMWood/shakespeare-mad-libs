Madlib Instruction

While most people have played MadLibs in their life, this version has a couple things you should be aware of before playing.

Full Page Play

When in full page play mode, the full section of the Shakespeare play is viewable. This mode gives context to each of the words being played and is similar to if you opened the book and played.

Single Word Play

When in Single Word mode, each word to fill out is presented one at a time as if it was being read to you. This keeps the context of the story out of the picture until the end.
